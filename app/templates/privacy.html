Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 August 2025

CVLift (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.
  • Uploaded Content: CVs, resumes, job descriptions, and related files you upload.
  • Usage Data: Logs, browser type, IP address, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process payments and deliver purchased credits.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with trusted service providers such as Stripe for payment processing, and with legal authorities when required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with our legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you signed in and to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & UK Data Protection)

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this policy, email us at cvlift@gmail.com.
